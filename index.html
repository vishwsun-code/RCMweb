Our Story

Built on Trust.
Sustained by Excellence.

Right Choice Medicare was founded with a singular mission: to make world-class medical equipment accessible to every healthcare facility — from the smallest diagnostic clinic to the largest tertiary care hospital.

Over 15 years, we have grown from a medical equipment distributor into an integrated healthcare infrastructure company, delivering turnkey hospital projects, international procurement bridges, government tender fulfillment, and lifetime biomedical support services.

With offices in Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad, and an international presence in China (Foshan) and Australia (NSW), we operate at the intersection of global sourcing capability and local service depth — serving India, Kenya, UAE, and across the MENA region.

Our team of biomedical engineers, procurement specialists, and clinical consultants work as one unified force — ensuring you get the right equipment at the right price with the right support, every single time.
